Reconstruction

Rebuild SL Metro

Post-disaster recovery platform. Focuses on coordinating cleanup and infrastructure rebuilding efforts in the metro area.

Developed by Team Metro Minds , Urban Informatics & Planning Dept., UOM.

How This Platform Helps:
  • Accelerated Assessment Citizens directly report damage with photos/location, creating a real-time comprehensive picture.
  • Data-Driven Funding Aggregates damage by GND to ensure resources go to the most affected areas based on actual data.
  • Faster Decisions Clear visualizations allow government/NGO leaders to identify priority areas instantly.
  • Community Collaboration Connects NGOs, volunteers, and communities to ensure coordinated relief.
View Recovery App
Food Security

AgriFlow

A centralized system to prevent agricultural waste by connecting farmers with market demand. Predicts supply gaps 3 months in advance.

Developed by Chamod Wismantha , Undergraduate, Urban Informatics & Planning Dept., UOM.

How This Platform Helps:
  • Demand Prediction Predicts market needs 3 months ahead to recommend optimal crops.
  • Farmers' Marketplace Connects farmers directly with buyers, eliminating middlemen.
  • Supply Management Real-time monitoring of shortages or oversupply at economic centers.

Crisis Management & Statistics

Response & Coordination

Disaster Management Centre (DMC)

National Authority
Visit DMC Portal

The central coordinating body for all disaster management activities in Sri Lanka. Responsible for issuing national alerts, coordinating between agencies, and producing the daily "Situation Report" (SitRep).

Key Data
  • Situation Reports
  • IDP & Casualty Counts
  • Safety Centers

Tri-Forces Operations

Operational Wing
Visit Navy Portal

The combined operational arm of the Army, Navy, and Air Force. They execute ground-level rescue missions, manage logistics in inaccessible areas, and provide air support for relief distribution.

Key Data
  • Rescue Logs
  • Aerial Surveillance
  • Access Reports
